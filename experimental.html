DESIGN
This theme is not supposed to be a perfect copy of LCARS from the old Star Trek series. this theme was created to fit a single screen 16x9 touch display or a control panel and designed to be a single page website. The text and buttons are larger than the original LCARS to make it easier to see and use from afar, and the rounded corners is too much of a hassle to style.
